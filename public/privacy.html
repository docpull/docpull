Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

docpull is a stateless API. We do not require accounts, logins, or personal information. When you call our API, we process the PDF URL you provide to perform extraction. We do not store PDF content after processing.

Payment data

Payments are processed on-chain via the x402 protocol on Base mainnet. We receive your wallet address as part of the payment transaction. This is public blockchain data. We do not store payment data beyond what is recorded on-chain.

Logs

We maintain standard server logs (request paths, timestamps, response codes) for operational purposes. Logs are retained for 30 days. We do not log PDF content or wallet private keys.

Third parties

We use the Coinbase Developer Platform facilitator for payment settlement. Railway for hosting. Neither receives your PDF content.
